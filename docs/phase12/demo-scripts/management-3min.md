# Management / VP · 3-minute Demo Script

**Persona**: VP Product / VP Programs — one level above domain team leaders
**Tab**: Status (primary, never leaves Status for this demo)
**Goal**: prove management walks off saying "I understand our EU launch posture in under 3 minutes, including what's uncertain."

---

## 0:00 – 0:30 · Verdict + confidence (30 s)

- Open `/status`.
- Hero card immediately shows **LIKELY OK · Confidence: Medium** (or whatever the pilot computes today).
- **Talking point**: *"The top-line verdict. Descriptive wording, not 'YES' — the tool is careful about its own authority (plan §0.3, IS NOT a legal signatory)."*

## 0:30 – 1:30 · 4 coverage metrics · Sprint 2 reconciliation (1:00)

- Four metric cards:
  - **Coverage score** e.g. "72 / 100" · project-scoped
  - **Verified applicable** · of X ACTIVE in registry
  - **Indicative applicable** · of Y SEED_UNVERIFIED in registry
  - **Pending authoring (in scope)** · of Z PLACEHOLDER in registry
- **Talking point**: *"Each number comes with a reconciliation sub-line so Status and Coverage no longer contradict each other. Sprint 2 UX-002 fix."*
- **Intentional**: note the "project-scoped" hint on Coverage score — project view ≠ registry view, and the tool is explicit.

## 1:30 – 2:15 · Top blockers + top deadlines · Sprint 1 time-awareness (45 s)

- **Top blockers** column: any MED / HIGH severity items (each is a Rules-tab deep-link).
- **Top deadlines** column: Sprint 1 UX-001 fix ensures past items read "14 months overdue" (red) and future items read "in 7 months" (neutral).
- Point at one overdue item (e.g. REG-AI-001 "Prohibited Practices", if this is 2026-04+).
- **Talking point**: *"Overdue is overdue. No '-14 months remaining' nonsense."*

## 2:15 – 3:00 · Countries at risk · honesty about FR / NL (45 s)

- **Countries at risk** column. For MY2027 BEV × DE/FR/NL pilot:
  - FR · **pending overlay — all rules placeholder (Phase 13+)** (amber)
  - NL · same (amber)
- **Talking point**: *"The tool shows FR and NL as at-risk because we haven't authored their national overlay yet. Not hidden, not over-promised. This is the honest market posture."*
- Scroll briefly up — show the **ScopeBanner** amber strip above: *"EU horizontal + DE overlay ACTIVE · FR / NL pending (Phase 13+)"*.

---

## Expected closing statement from stakeholder

> *"3 minutes, honest picture. I know our DE launch is on track, FR / NL need more work, and I can see what the blockers are without asking my program manager. Can you send this as a monthly export?"*

(PDF export via the Export button is Sprint 10 verification scope.)

## Sprint 8 dry-run checklist

- [ ] Hero verdict shown in < 20 s
- [ ] Four coverage metrics each show registry-reconciliation sub-line
- [ ] At least one overdue deadline labelled correctly (Sprint 1 UX-001)
- [ ] FR / NL appear in Countries at risk with "pending_overlay" reason
- [ ] ScopeBanner visible at all times
- [ ] Total time ≤ 3:30

§02 What it is

A compliance-navigation toolNavigation, not legal advice

A clickable, exportable, back-plannable workbench for the work of taking a Chinese OEM into the EU market.

  • Compliance-navigation tool for Chinese OEMs entering the EU market
  • Input: project configuration. Output: applicable-rules list + timeline
  • Positioned as a navigation aid — not a substitute for legal counsel or TÜV judgement
§03 What's inside

Three things users seeThree things users see

One project configuration, three audiences at once: homologation engineers, team leaders, and program management.

  • Applicable-rules list (EU / UNECE / member-state)
  • SOP-anchored task timeline
  • Owner dashboard grouped by team + one-click evidence-pack export
§04 Why it matters

Three shifts for the organisationThree shifts

Not an extra document — three familiar problems reframed.

  • Rule discovery: from reading raw texts to per-project checklisting
  • Compliance deadlines: from last-minute alerts to back-planned reminders
  • Cross-team work: from parallel spreadsheets to a shared checklist
